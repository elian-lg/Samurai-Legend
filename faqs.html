Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What makes the samurai sword special?
    A: The samurai sword, or katana, is revered for its exceptional craftsmanship. Each sword is a masterpiece, forged by skilled blacksmiths and designed for both function and artistic expression.
  • Q: Can I customize my own samurai sword?
    A: At the moment, we offer pre-designed models. However, we are working on providing customization options in the future.
  • Q: How are the swords crafted?
    A: Our swords are crafted by skilled artisans using traditional Japanese sword-making techniques. Each sword undergoes a meticulous process to ensure quality and precision.
  • Q: Do you offer international shipping?
    A: Yes, we do! We offer international shipping to bring the power of the samurai sword to warriors around the world.
  • Q: Is the website age-restricted?
    A: Yes, due to the nature of the content, we have an age verification process to ensure users are 18 years or older.
